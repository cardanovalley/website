About cardano valley

Have you ever had the feeling of discovering a genius artist that deserves more attention?

By living in a developing country we see that quite often: Plenty of artist that have been producing great work for years, but their message can't be spread enough (not too fair, right?). Here’s the thing, we really believe NFTs are an incredible tool to fix that.

What we dream about is to work with them, help them curate their work and show their creations to the Cardano Community, so we all together can enjoy their awesome art and help them grow, as we all grow too.

FAQ

Why buy a Cardagotchi?

There are two main reasons to buy a Cardagotchi. If you like them or if you want to promote emerging artists. Income from Cardagotchis sells is going to be used to fund another collections that will be made for emerging artists.

Weren't there 10,000 Cardagotchis before?

Yes. We created 10,000 designs but we only minted about 2,280 before our policy expired. We think that brings more value and importance to those we already sold and the people that already supported us. Shorting the pre-sale process gives us time to focus on helping emerging artists with their collections which is the main target.

Do you already have artists working with you?

Yes. First of all, one of our founders is, himself, an emerging artist. But, furthermore, we are already working with two emerging artist we choose and we have some artists on hold before we polish our process.
