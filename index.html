About cardano valley

Have you ever had the feeling of discovering a genius artist that deserves more attention?

In our circles we see this quite often: Plenty of artist that have been producing great work for years, but their message can't be spread enough (not fair, right?). Here’s the thing, we really believe NFTs are an incredible tool to fix that.

We dream about working with them to help them curate their work and show their creations to the Cardano Community, so we can all together enjoy their awesome art and help them grow, as we all grow too.

FAQ

Why buy a Cardagotchi?

There are two main reasons to buy a Cardagotchi. Because you like them or because you want to promote emerging artists. Income from Cardagotchi sells is going to be used to fund other collections that will be made by emerging artists.

Weren't there 10,000 Cardagotchis before?

Yes. We created 10,000 designs but we only minted about 2,280 before our policy expired. We think that brings more value and importance to those already sold and to the people that already supported us. Shorting the pre-sale process gives us time to focus on helping emerging artists with their collections which is the main target.

Do you already have artists working with you?

Yes. First of all, one of our founders is, himself, an emerging artist. But, furthermore, we are already working with two other emerging artist we chose and we are keeping some artists on hold before we polish our process.
